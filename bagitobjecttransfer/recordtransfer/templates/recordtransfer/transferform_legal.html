By completing and submitting this form you agree to give the National Centre for Truth and Reconciliation Archives legal, physical, and intellectual control and responsibility over the materials you submit for the purpose of allowing the NCTR Archives to inspect, research and appraise the material for a potential donation to the NCTR.
